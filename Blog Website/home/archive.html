About PHFOODLOG

We are passionate food lovers dedicated to bringing you the authentic flavors of Manila’s everyday cuisine. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches, delicious dinners, and mouthwatering desserts, we cover it all. Our mission is to share traditional Filipino dishes that you can enjoy at home or discover in the heart of Manila. Whether you’re craving classic tapsilog for breakfast, savory adobo for lunch, or sweet leche flan for dessert, PHFOODBLOG is your go-to source for recipes and food stories. Join us as we explore Manila’s rich culinary heritage, one meal at a time!
